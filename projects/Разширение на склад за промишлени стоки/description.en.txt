The single-storey warehouse for industrial goods is located in the Northern Industrial Zone of Burgas and is directly connected to the existing warehouse building. The layout provides for rack storage of goods on Euro pallets and loading and unloading operations using an electric forklift. The building has a steel structure, and the façades are finished with horizontally oriented insulated sandwich panels. The roof is also constructed with insulated sandwich panels and polycarbonate rooflight strips, which provide natural daylight to the interior.
