The aircraft maintenance and service facility is designed for Airbus A320 aircraft. The complex includes a hangar for the simultaneous maintenance of up to three aircraft, a paint hangar and a multifunctional administrative and staff amenities building. The latter accommodates offices, training rooms, changing rooms, a canteen and residential units for temporary accommodation. The buildings are connected by enclosed links, while the administrative block is organised around a roof-lit atrium and surrounded by water features. Covered parking, bicycle spaces and recreation areas are also provided.
