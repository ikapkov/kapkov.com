The single-storey, open-sided warehouse is intended for the storage of construction materials and is located in the industrial zone of Sliven. An outdoor storage area is provided adjacent to the building, with designated zones for storage and the movement of loading and unloading equipment. The building has a steel structure and façade cladding made of insulated sandwich panels. The layout includes rack storage, entrances on both sides and aisles for manoeuvring. The new storage areas support the operations of the existing retail facility.
