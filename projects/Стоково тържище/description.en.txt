The wholesale market is located in the Northern Industrial Zone of Burgas and comprises four separate warehouse buildings intended for the storage and wholesale trade of agricultural products. Each building is organised into repeated functional modules, each comprising an individual storage unit, an office and sanitary facilities. The buildings have mixed prefabricated structural systems, while the façades are clad with horizontally oriented insulated sandwich panels. Separate pedestrian and loading entrances, parking spaces and convenient access to all functional areas are provided.
